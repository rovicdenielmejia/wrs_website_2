Pricing FAQ

Is there a minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts required. Project-based work can be completed without ongoing commitments. Monthly plans offer the best value for regular hiring.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer satisfaction guarantees on all placements. If a candidate doesn't work out within the guarantee period, we provide a replacement at no additional cost.

How does payment work?

50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon candidate acceptance. Enterprise clients may arrange monthly billing.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
