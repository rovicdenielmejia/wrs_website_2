Our Process

Structured Recruitment Process

A systematic approach to quality hiring

Our proven recruitment methodology ensures consistent, high-quality placements. Each step is designed to reduce hiring risks and improve long-term retention.

The 6-Stage Process

How We Deliver Quality Placements

1

Workforce Planning

We begin by understanding your business goals, team dynamics, and long-term growth plans. This ensures alignment between the role requirements and organizational objectives.

  • Role analysis and job specification development
  • Organizational fit assessment
  • Success criteria definition
  • Timeline and hiring strategy
2

Talent Sourcing

We leverage multiple channels to attract qualified candidates, from our established network to targeted job boards and professional platforms.

  • Active candidate database mining
  • Job board optimization and posting
  • Social media and professional network outreach
  • Referral program activation
3

Initial Screening

All applications undergo rigorous pre-screening to verify qualifications, experience, and cultural alignment before advancing to your consideration.

  • Resume screening and verification
  • Phone/video preliminary interviews
  • Skills assessment evaluation
  • Salary expectation alignment
4

In-Depth Evaluation

Shortlisted candidates complete comprehensive assessments to validate their capabilities, personality traits, and problem-solving abilities.

  • Technical competency testing
  • Behavioral and situational interviews
  • Reference checks with previous employers
  • Background verification
5

Client Presentation

You receive detailed candidate profiles with our professional recommendations, enabling informed decision-making for final interviews.

  • Comprehensive candidate reports
  • Shortlist presentation with comparisons
  • Interview scheduling coordination
  • Interview preparation guidance for candidates
6

Placement & Onboarding

Our support continues beyond the offer letter. We ensure smooth transitions and provide follow-up to confirm successful integration.

  • Offer negotiation support
  • Onboarding checklist guidance
  • 30-60-90 day check-ins
  • Replacement guarantee period

Our Commitment

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind every placement. If a candidate does not work out within our guarantee period due to performance or fit issues, we provide a replacement at no additional cost.

  • Replacement guarantee on all placements
  • Ongoing support throughout the hiring process
  • Transparent communication at every stage
  • Post-placement follow-up and support
