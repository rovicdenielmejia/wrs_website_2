## 2. Website Purpose

The WRS website serves two primary functions:

### Information Hub
- Services, solutions, pricing, about, FAQ, blogs, platform information
- Company information, team details, testimonials
- Educational content for employers and job seekers

### Hiring & Recruitment
- Job listings for job seekers
- Employer solutions and consultation
- Contact/consultation forms for both employers and candidates
- Career support services

---

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary: Philippine SMEs
- Growing businesses needing recruitment support
- Companies without dedicated HR/recruitment teams
- Businesses scaling and needing workforce planning

### Secondary: Job Seekers
- Professionals seeking better career opportunities
- Candidates needing resume and interview support
- Individuals looking for remote work options
